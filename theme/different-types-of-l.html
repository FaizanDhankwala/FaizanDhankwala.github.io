The BMW i8 is a plug-in hybrid sports car that was made by BMW. The i8 is part of BMW's electric cars. it was made in 2015 model. BMW i8 has a 7.1 kWh lithium-ion battery pack that delivers an all-electric range of 37 km.. Its design is heavily influenced by the BMW M1 Homage concept car, which is actually not street legal. The i8 is a mid-engined sports car prior to the i8: the BMW M1. BMW will cease the production of the BMW i8 in April 2020 because they are limited edition.
