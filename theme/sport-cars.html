Some histroy about the LaFerrari is based on findings from testing of the FXX development prototype and on research being conducted by the Millechili Project at the University of Modena. Association with the Millechili Project led to speculation during development that the car would weigh under 1,000 kg was claimed. Only 499 units were produced, and each cost more than 1 million US dollars.
